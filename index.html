About H.M.C. Traders

H.M.C. Traders is a leading ginger processing and trading company based in Karnataka, India. We specialize in the procurement, processing, and distribution of fresh and dry ginger across various states in India.

Our state-of-the-art washing and processing facilities ensure the highest quality standards for our products. Located at Annavatti to Shivamogga Main Road, CHIKKAIDAGODU-577413 Soraba Tq, Karnataka, we are strategically positioned to serve customers across the region.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality ginger products while maintaining sustainable and ethical trading practices that benefit both our customers and local farming communities.
